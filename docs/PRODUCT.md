# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Laboratory professionals, biochemistry and molecular biology researchers, and students. They operate in active lab environments, often under bright overhead lighting, and need to perform rapid, reliable, and error-free calculations at the bench or while analyzing experimental datasets.

## Product Purpose
CalcuLab provides a browser-based suite of molecular biology and biochemistry calculators. It exists to replace slow, error-prone manual calculations and disorganized spreadsheets with verified, instantaneous, and SI-unit-aware calculators. Success looks like error-free solutions, fast qPCR analysis, and a frictionless benchtop companion.

## Brand Personality
Precise, utilitarian, and reliable. The voice is that of an expert laboratory assistant—no unnecessary fluff, direct to the point, and focused entirely on accuracy and speed.

## Anti-references
- Bloated SaaS dashboards with low-contrast, tiny, light-gray text.
- Sketchy, unaligned, or amateurish interfaces that fail to inspire professional confidence.
- Overly decorated websites with distracting animations or non-functional visual noise.

## Design Principles
- **Bench-Ready Clarity**: Contrast and sizing must be optimized for glancing at a screen from a distance or under harsh, reflective laboratory lighting.
- **Failsafe Inputs**: Prevent calculation mistakes by validating inputs immediately and clearly marking errors and unit changes.
- **Immediate Results**: Minimize the interaction path. Calculations should happen instantly, and results must be grouped clearly and be easily copyable.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Target WCAG 2.1 Level AA compliance.
- Maintain high-contrast text ratios (>= 4.5:1) for all critical scientific metrics and values.
- Clean layout supporting browser zoom and keyboard-only modal navigation.
